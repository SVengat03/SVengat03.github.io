Bot Overview

This bot is a very hobbyist bot, initially to just have fun doing Python, now to provide good services to our very best.

This bot is made by hobbyist and amateur bot developers, so don't expect it to be very "premium" and high class -- it is being developed constantly and you might get random errors a bit frequently, but is generally fine.
